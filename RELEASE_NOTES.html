What's New in 26.06.5

  • New tool — PopBar: Select text in any app and a little capsule of actions pops up right at your cursor. Pick one and the AI answer streams in live, formatted as rich text (headings, lists, code).
  • Your own actions: Add, edit, and reorder actions — each can use its own model. Bring your own provider key (DeepSeek, OpenAI, Doubao, Qwen, Ollama).
  • Comfortable windows: Drag a result anywhere, let it grow to fit long answers, dial the text size to taste, and pin several results open side by side.

26.06.5 更新内容

  • 新增工具 · 划词胶囊 PopBar:在任意 App 里选中文字,光标旁就会弹出一排操作胶囊。点一下,AI 回答就实时流式显示,并按富文本渲染(标题、列表、代码)。
  • 动作随你定:可添加、编辑、排序动作,每个动作可单独选模型;自带 provider 密钥(DeepSeek、OpenAI、豆包、通义千问、Ollama)。
  • 窗口更顺手:结果窗口可拖动、可随内容自动变高、可调字号,还能同时固定多个结果并排查看。

What's New in 26.06.4

  • New tool — Memory: See memory pressure at a glance, plus a stacked bar showing how your RAM is split up (Wired / App / Compressed / Cached / Free) and Swap on its own. Every metric has a plain-language explanation, so you don't need to know the jargon.
  • New — Dashboard: A glanceable overview that's now the landing page. Cards for Memory, Battery, Wake Locks, Ports, Disk, and Swap — each one click away from its full tool.

26.06.4 更新内容

  • 新增工具 · 内存:一眼看清内存压力,并用堆叠条展示内存是怎么分配的(锁定 / 应用 / 已压缩 / 已缓存 / 空闲),交换区单独列出。每一项指标都配了大白话解释,不用懂术语也看得明白。
  • 新增 · 概览 Dashboard:启动即见的总览页,内存、电池、唤醒占用、端口、磁盘、交换区都做成卡片,点一下就进入对应工具。
